This is the closest station
We are collecting the daily reports from the German Environmental Protection Agency who is measuring the air quality at different points in the country. This tool helps you to find your closest station and give you the possibility to follow it by receiving email alerts or by subscribing to its RSS feed.

Say hello to our new bot

Our service is now available on Facebook Messenger too. You can ask for air quality at a location and subscribe to station alerts directly from Messenger. Start the chat by saying hi !
